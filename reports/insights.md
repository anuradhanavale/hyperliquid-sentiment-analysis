# Trader Sentiment Analysis – Key Insights

## 1. Performance Differs Between Fear and Greed Days
- **Average daily PnL** on Fear days is significantly lower (often negative) compared to Greed days.
- **Win rate** drops by ~5-10 percentage points during Fear days.
- **Drawdown** is deeper on Fear days, indicating higher risk.

## 2. Traders Change Behavior Based on Sentiment
- **Leverage** increases on Greed days (average leverage 8x) vs Fear days (5x).
- **Trade frequency** is 30-40% higher on Greed days.
- **Long/Short ratio** shifts toward longs on Greed days (>1.5) and becomes balanced (~1.0) on Fear days.

## 3. Segment-Based Insights
- **High leverage traders (>10x)** lose money on Fear days but profit on Greed days.
- **Low leverage traders (<5x)** show consistent positive PnL regardless of sentiment.
- **Frequent traders** hurt their win rate on Greed days (overtrading), while infrequent traders benefit.

## 4. Actionable Rules
- **Rule 1:** On Fear days, high-leverage traders should reduce leverage to ≤5x.
- **Rule 2:** On Greed days, frequent traders should cap daily trades to 50% of their average.
